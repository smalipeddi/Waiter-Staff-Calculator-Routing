Waiter staff calculator is used by the waiters to calculate their daily waiter tips they earn from the cutomers as a part of the tips given by them for every meal the customer has atthe restaurant.
This app has three tabs- Home, New meals and My Earnings.
1. Home tab displays the default main page
2. The New Menu displays the details of the Meal details and customer charges.
3. The My Earnings tab displays the Earnings for waiter, the total tip earned for from all the meals.
